## The longer version

My name is Obed Favour Chukwuemeka. I'm a Growth Marketer and AI Automation Expert based in Lagos — and for the past 4+ years I've been obsessed with one question: *what actually makes brands grow?*

Not theory. Not vanity metrics. Real, compounding, sustainable growth.
